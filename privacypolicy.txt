Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Thank you for using Soggy Biscuit. This Privacy Policy explains what information the application collects, how it is used, and how it is stored.

Information We Collect

The application collects only the information necessary to provide its multiplayer functionality and to improve the application.

The application may collect:

A display name chosen by the player during gameplay. This name can be changed at any time.
Gameplay information, including game date, game duration, accepted challenges, and other game-related statistics.
An anonymous unique identifier generated by Firebase Anonymous Authentication, which is used solely to provide multiplayer functionality and maintain game sessions.

The application does not intentionally collect sensitive personal information.

How We Use Information

The collected information is used to:

Operate multiplayer game sessions.
Maintain game functionality.
Improve the application and user experience.
Generate anonymous usage statistics and analytics.

The collected information is not sold or shared with third parties for advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

The application uses Google Firebase services, including:

Firebase Anonymous Authentication
Firebase Realtime Database

These services may process data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

Gameplay data and anonymous authentication identifiers are stored securely using Google Firebase services.

Data Retention

Gameplay data, including display names used during games and gameplay statistics, are currently retained indefinitely for operational and analytical purposes.

Anonymous authentication identifiers generated by Firebase Anonymous Authentication are also retained while they are required for the operation of the application. Because Firebase Anonymous Authentication does not require users to provide personally identifiable information and the stored gameplay data cannot reasonably be linked to an identifiable individual, anonymous gameplay data may continue to be retained indefinitely.

Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect stored information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

Children's Privacy

The application is not directed toward children under the age required by applicable law and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

Because the application uses anonymous authentication and does not require users to provide personally identifiable information, gameplay data cannot reasonably be associated with a specific individual. Consequently, anonymous gameplay records cannot be individually identified or deleted upon request.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last updated" date will be revised accordingly.
