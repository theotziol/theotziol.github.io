Privacy policy
We collect limited information necessary to provide multiplayer functionality and improve the application.

Information we collect may include:

The display name chosen by the player during gameplay.
Gameplay statistics, such as game duration, accepted challenges, and similar anonymous usage information.
A unique account identifier generated by Firebase Authentication when authentication is used.

This information is used solely to operate the multiplayer features and to analyze overall application usage.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information.

Users who create an account may request deletion of their account and associated data through the application or by contacting us at jiolas@hotmail.com
